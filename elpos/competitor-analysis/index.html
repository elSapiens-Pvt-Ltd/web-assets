Competitor Profiles

Detailed profiles of each POS system, their focus, and key differentiators.

Petpooja

India
Focus: Restaurants 50,000+ outlets

India's leading restaurant POS with deep food aggregator integrations (Swiggy, Zomato, Dunzo). Strong in dine-in, takeaway, and delivery management.

  • Swiggy/Zomato/Dunzo direct integration
  • Captain app for tableside ordering
  • Anti-theft module (monitors suspicious voids/discounts)
  • Central kitchen management for chains
  • 80+ reports with GST compliance
  • Recipe-level ingredient tracking

POSist (Restroworks)

India + Middle East
Focus: Restaurant Chains Enterprise grade

Enterprise restaurant management platform for multi-brand chains. Strong in analytics, food cost management, and multi-outlet operations.

  • Multi-brand, multi-outlet management
  • AI-driven demand forecasting
  • Actual vs theoretical food cost tracking
  • Vendor performance scoring
  • Cockpit app for owners (mobile dashboard)
  • Automated report scheduling via email

Square POS

US
Focus: Multi-vertical Free base tier

Complete business ecosystem — POS, payments, banking, payroll, online store, lending. Strongest in micro/small business with zero monthly fees on base plan.

  • Free POS software (pay only processing fees)
  • Square Banking + Capital (lending based on sales)
  • Vertical-specific: Retail, Restaurant, Appointments
  • Square Payroll integration
  • Instant bank deposits
  • Developer-friendly APIs

Toast POS

US

Purpose-built for restaurants with industry-leading features. xtraCHEF invoice scanning, handheld tableside ordering, and deep food cost analytics.

Shopify POS

Best-in-class omnichannel retail — unified online + in-store commerce. Buy online pick up in store, return anywhere, unified inventory and customer profiles.

Advanced inventory management with supplier catalog integration. Strongest in specialty retail (bike shops, electronics, apparel) and fine dining.

Clover POS

Focus: Multi-vertical All-in-one hardware

Premium hardware + software bundles distributed via banks. App marketplace with 300+ extensions. Strong for service businesses (salons, contractors).

  • Premium all-in-one hardware design
  • 300+ apps in Clover App Market
  • Distributed via banks (Bank of America, etc.)

Marg ERP

India
Focus: Retail / Wholesale / Pharma 500+ reports

Desktop-first ERP with 30+ industry variants. Dominates Indian pharma distribution, FMCG wholesale, and manufacturing. Extremely fast keyboard-driven billing.

  • 30+ industry-specific variants (pharma, auto parts, steel, etc.)
  • Batch + expiry date tracking (pharma/FMCG)
  • Manufacturing/BOM module
  • 500-1000+ MIS reports
  • Auto-GST filing + GSTR-1/3B/9
  • Speed billing with keyboard shortcuts

Vyapar

India
Focus: Small Business Billing Mobile-first

Ultra-simple mobile billing app for India's informal retail sector. Digitized khata (credit ledger) for millions of small shops. WhatsApp invoice sharing.

  • WhatsApp integration (invoices, payment reminders)
  • Khata digitization (credit/debit ledger)
  • Extremely simple UX (designed for non-tech users)
  • Free tier available
  • E-invoice and e-way bill support
  • Bank statement import for reconciliation

Gap Analysis — What elPOS is Missing

Features every serious POS has that elPOS needs to build, prioritized by competitive necessity.

P0 — Ship Blockers (Cannot sell without these)

    Staff / Employee Management
    Clock in/out, shift tracking, sales per cashier, role-based POS access, manager approval for voids/discounts
    Present in: ALL competitors except Vyapar
  • P0
    Returns & Refunds Flow
    Return against original invoice, partial returns, refund to original payment method or store credit, return reason tracking
    Present in: ALL competitors
  • P0
    Discount / Promotion Engine
    Item-level and order-level discounts, percentage or flat, Buy-X-Get-Y, combo deals, coupon codes, happy hour pricing
    Present in: ALL competitors
  • P0
    Reports Dashboard
    At minimum: sales summary, item-wise, category-wise, hourly, payment method, tax, discount, void reports
    Present in: ALL competitors (15+ reports minimum)
  • P0
    Full Customer Management
    Searchable customer list, purchase history, outstanding balance, customer profile page
    Present in: ALL competitors
  • P0
    Audit Trail
    Every void, discount, refund, and settlement logged with who/when/why

elPOS Competitive Advantage

Where elPOS wins against every competitor.

Multi-Vertical (Unique)

No competitor covers retail + restaurant + laundry/service in one product. Petpooja is restaurant-only. Shopify is retail-only. elPOS does all three with a unified cart, payments, and customer system.

Offline-First (Rare)

Most competitors need internet. In tier-2/3 India where connectivity is unreliable, elPOS working fully offline with automatic sync is a massive differentiator. Only Marg and Vyapar match this.

Ecosystem Integration

Petpooja is standalone. elPOS plugs into inventory, accounting, CRM, messaging — a unified business platform. Square does this in the US, but nobody does it in India.

Laundry / Service POS

There is no serious organized POS for laundry in India. Most use paper tags and manual registers. 257 garment types, 12 services, per-piece condition tracking with photos — wide open market.

Multi-Country Ready

The ecosystem's geolocations service already has currency, tax, and formatting data for every country. While Petpooja/Marg are India-locked, elPOS can expand to UAE, SE Asia, Africa.

No Hardware Lock-In

Petpooja pushes their terminals. Toast requires Toast hardware. Clover needs Clover devices. elPOS runs on any tablet, laptop, phone, or dedicated terminal — zero vendor lock-in.
