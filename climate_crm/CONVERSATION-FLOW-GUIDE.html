💬 Journey 1: Contact Conversation

conversation_type = 'contact'

This is for new leads and prospects. The goal is to profile them and eventually create an opportunity.

👤 Contact WITHOUT Account

New lead - We don't have their business details yet

  • 1

    Lead Arrives

    Someone messages us on WhatsApp, fills a form, or calls.

    Stage: NEW
  • 2

    You Reach Out

    Call them or send a message to start the conversation.

    Stage: ATTEMPTED
  • 3

    They Respond

    Great! They replied or you spoke to them.

    Stage: CONTACTED
  • 4

    Create Their Profile

    Add their business details (company name, address, GST). This is called "profiling".

    Stage: NURTURING (stays open)
  • 5

    Create Opportunity

    When they're ready to buy, create a quote for them.

    Stage: CONVERTED (closes)

💡 Key Point

Creating a profile does NOT close the conversation. You continue talking to them until you create an opportunity or mark as unqualified.

🏢 Contact WITH Account

Existing customer - We already have their business details

  • 1

    Lead Arrives

    Someone we already know contacts us again.

    Stage: NEW
  • 2

    You Reach Out

    Call them or send a message.

    Stage: ATTEMPTED
  • 3

    They Respond

    You're now in conversation with them.

    Stage: CONTACTED
  • 4

    Create Opportunity Directly

    No need to profile - just create a quote! Their details are already in the system.

    Stage: CONVERTED (closes)

💡 Key Point

When you create an opportunity, this conversation closes and a NEW "opportunity" conversation opens to track the deal until order.

💰 Journey 2: Opportunity Conversation

conversation_type = 'opportunity'

This is for tracking active deals/quotes. The goal is to close the sale and place an order.

⚡ How is an Opportunity Conversation Created?

An Opportunity Conversation is automatically created by the system when you create an opportunity/quote from a Contact Conversation.

💰 Opportunity Conversation Journey

Track the deal from quote to order

  • 1

    Opportunity Conversation Created

    When you create an opportunity from a Contact conversation, a NEW conversation is automatically created with type = 'opportunity'.

    Stage: NEW
  • 2

    Follow Up on Quote

    Call or message the customer to follow up on the quote you sent.

    Stage: ATTEMPTED
  • 3

    Customer Responds

    They reply about the quote - maybe asking questions, negotiating price, or ready to order.

    Stage: CONTACTED
  • 4

    Place Order

    Customer is ready! Create the order to complete the sale.

    Stage: CONVERTED (closes)

💡 Key Point

Opportunity conversations are shorter - they skip profiling since the customer already has an account. The only goal is to close the sale!

📄 Two Types of Conversations

When you create an opportunity, the system creates a NEW conversation to track that deal separately.

💬 Contact Conversation

For new leads and prospects

  • Purpose: First contact with a lead, profiling, qualification
  • Goal: Get their details, understand needs, create opportunity
  • Ends when: Opportunity is created OR marked unqualified

💰 Opportunity Conversation

For active deals waiting for order

  • Purpose: Track a specific deal/quote until order is placed
  • Goal: Follow up on the quote, negotiate, close the sale
  • Ends when: Order is placed OR marked unqualified

⚡ Automatic Stage Changes

The system automatically moves conversations through stages based on what happens. You don't need to manually change stages!

When This Happens... Stage Becomes... Conversation Status
New message/call comes in NEW Opens
You send a message or make a call ATTEMPTED Stays Open
Customer replies or call is answered CONTACTED Stays Open
You schedule a follow-up NURTURING Stays Open
You create profile / link account NURTURING Stays Open
You create an opportunity/quote CONVERTED Closes
Order is placed CONVERTED Closes
You mark as unqualified UNQUALIFIED Closes

📅 What Happens to Pending Follow-ups?

When transferring, you choose what happens to your scheduled follow-ups:

🔁
Transfer to new agent

Follow-ups will be reassigned to the receiving agent

✅
Mark as done

Follow-ups will be marked as completed

🗑
Delete

Follow-ups will be permanently deleted

💡 Transfer Chain

All transferred conversations are linked together. You can view the full transfer history to see who handled the conversation before and after you. This helps maintain context and accountability across the team.

🚨 Mandatory Action Popup

When you try to switch to another conversation, the system may ask you to take action first.

⚠ Why do I see this popup?

The popup appears when you try to switch to another conversation and the current conversation is in CONTACTED stage set via a call (outgoing call answered or incoming call answered).

Important: This popup only appears for call-based contact, NOT for message replies.

Since you already spoke to this customer on a call, you must take one of these actions before moving to another lead:

📅
Schedule Follow-up

Set a reminder to contact them later (moves to NURTURING)

👤
Create Profile / Link Account

Save their business details if no account yet (moves to NURTURING)

🚫
Mark as Unqualified

Close the conversation if they're not a good fit (moves to UNQUALIFIED)

Why is this mandatory? When you've had actual contact with a customer, we need to track what happens next. This ensures no leads fall through the cracks and every conversation has a clear outcome.
